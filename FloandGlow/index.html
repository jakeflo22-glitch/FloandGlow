FAQ

A few quick answers. If you’re still not sure, just text or email us — we’re happy to talk it through.

Do you only clean exterior windows?

Yes. Flo & Glow is currently focused on exterior glass only. That keeps things simple and fast, and lets us use our pure water-fed pole system for most of the work.

Do I need to be home for the cleaning?

Not always. For exterior-only work, as long as we’ve agreed on the scope and can access the areas we need, we can clean while you’re out and text you when we’re done.

Will you still use ladders sometimes?

Yes, if needed. Some windows require ladders, but our goal is to safely do as much as possible from the ground using water-fed poles and smart positioning.

How often should I have my exterior windows cleaned?

Most homes are fine with 1–2 exterior cleans per year. If you’re near trees, construction dust, or a busy road, you may want more frequent visits.
